One fisherman learns a lesson

FISHERMAN 1: Oh dear! I lost my fishing rod! Help! Help!

GOD: (Floats to the fisherman.) What can I help you with?

FISHERMAN 1: I lost my fishing rod in the forest! How can my family eat!

GOD: I can help you. (Goes into the forest and finds a golden fishing rod.) Is this fishing rod yours?

FISHERMAN 1: No. Mine was a wooden one.

GOD: Let me try again. (Goes into the forest and finds a silver fishing rod.) Is this yours?

FISHERMAN 1: No. Mine was a wooden one.

GOD: The forest is dark so I couldn't find yours, but let me try again. (Goes into forest and finds a wooden fishing rod.) Is this yours?

FISHERMAN 1: Yes! This is mine! Thanks for finding mine!

GOD: As a reward, I will give you the golden and the siver one to you.

FISHERMAN 1: (Runs to the town.) I am rich! Yay! I can buy everything in this village!

FISHERMAN 2: (Thinks, "I can pretend I lost my fishing rod." Runs to forest and drops fishing rod and comes to god.) I lost my fishing rod! Can you help me?

GOD: Yes. (Goes into the forest and finds a golden fishing rod.) Is this fishing rod yours?

FISHERMAN 2: Yes! This in mine!

GOD: LIAR! As a result, I will not give the golden fishing rod and your fishing rod to you.

FISHERMAN 2: (Cries "Give back my fishing rod." and runs away never to be seen.)
